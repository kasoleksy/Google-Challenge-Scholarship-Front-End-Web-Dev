Adelie penguin

The Adelie Penguin is the smallest and most widely distributed species of Penguin in the Southern Ocean and is one of only two species of Penguin found on the Antarctic mainland.

  • Scientific Name: Pygoscelis adeliae
  • Group: Bird
  • Life Span: 10 - 20 years
  • Fun Fact: Eats up to 2kg of food per day!

Like all species of Penguin, the Adelie Penguin is a highly sociable animal, gathering in large groups known as colonies, which often number thousands of Penguin individuals. Adelie Penguins are also known to hunt in groups as it is thought to reduce the risk of being eaten by hungry predators.
